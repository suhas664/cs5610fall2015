Open Source Projects

The project is aimed at building a website that helps people find other like minded people with the same interests to work on a project. Anyone and everyone can register and either post ideas of projects they want to work on or join projects that have been posted by other people. This service also lists people and projects that are geographically closer.

* Those are not the numbers we are at right now, BUT WE WOULD DEFINITELY LIKE TO GET THERE
